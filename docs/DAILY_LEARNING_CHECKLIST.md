## Quick Reference: Common Patterns

### Loss Patterns to Watch For
1. **Improver Demoted** - We identified improver but picked different horse
   - Action: Increase improver boost weights

2. **Winner Not in Top 5** - Winner ranked #6+
   - Action: Review why winner scored low, increase relevant signal

3. **Market Favorite Won** - Market backed it, we didn't
   - Action: Review market divergence, possibly increase market weights

4. **Field Composition Error** - Winner not analyzed at all
   - Action: Fix field verification system, not a model issue

5. **Heavy Going** - Race in heavy going, unpredictable
   - Action: Acceptable loss, heavy going penalty already applied

### Win Patterns to Reinforce
1. **Improver Boost Applied** - Improver flag + boost = winner
   - Action: Maintain or increase improver boost

2. **Class Drop** - Horse dropped class and won
   - Action: Maintain or increase class_drop_bonus

3. **Form Velocity** - Horse on improving trend
   - Action: Maintain or increase form_velocity_bonus

4. **Trainer/Jockey Hot Form** - Combo on win streak
   - Action: Maintain hot form bonuses

5. **Course Specialist** - Horse with course wins
   - Action: Maintain course_bonus weight

1. Review 5-10 races per day (focus on biggest losses and best wins)
2. Complete relevant sections for each race
3. Look for patterns across multiple races
4. Make weight adjustment decisions on Sunday based on weekly summary
5. Track changes and monitor impact over following week
