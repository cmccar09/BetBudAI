BetBudAI Exit Plan

This document outlines what targets to hit before a sale, how buyers will value the platform, and what subscriber/ROI milestones support higher valuation multiples.

Current Pricing Model

Use your live public pricing as the baseline for all valuation math.

Premium: EUR 9.99/mo
VIP: EUR 49.99/mo

Formula: ARR = (Premium subs x 9.99 x 12) + (VIP subs x 49.99 x 12)

Buyer Lens

  • Can this grow predictably with low churn?
  • Is ROI real, consistent, and auditable?
  • Is operational risk low (automation, uptime, data pipeline)?
  • Can a new owner run it without founder dependency?

Exit Timing Trigger

Start sale process when all are true for at least 3 consecutive months:

  • MRR above EUR 25k
  • Net churn below 4%
  • 90-day ROI positive with published audit trail
  • No critical pipeline failures affecting customer trust

ROI Standards Buyers Expect

  • Consistency: Positive returns across 30/90/180-day windows.
  • Risk control: Max drawdown clearly reported and capped.
  • Auditability: Every pick traceable to timestamped input/output.
  • No cherry-picking: Full published history, including losing periods.

Subscriber Benchmarks

  • Free-to-paid conversion: target 5-9%
  • Monthly paid churn: target under 5%
  • VIP upgrade rate from Premium: target 12-20%
  • LTV/CAC: target greater than 3.0x

Risk Discounts To Avoid

  • Single founder dependency without SOPs
  • Pipeline outages affecting picks delivery
  • No legal/terms clarity around betting disclaimers
  • Unclear user data compliance posture

If You Have Mostly Free Subscribers

A large free user base can still support a sale, but buyers will apply a discount unless conversion and monetization are proven and stable.

Scenario What Buyers See Likely Valuation Impact What To Improve Before Sale
Mostly free, low conversion (under 3%) Audience value is unproven and revenue is fragile Typically lower range, around 1.0x-2.5x ARR Improve onboarding, paid triggers, and upgrade journeys; prove 90-day conversion lift
Mostly free, improving conversion (3-6%) Early signs of monetization fit with upside Usually base range, around 2.0x-4.0x ARR Show cohort retention and reduced paid churn across at least 2-3 quarters
Mostly free, strong conversion (6%+) Efficient acquisition funnel with clear revenue engine Can approach growth range, around 3.5x-5.5x ARR Protect ROI quality, improve VIP upgrade rate, and document repeatable growth playbooks

For free-heavy products, buyers focus on conversion trend quality, not just total user count. A stable conversion curve can materially increase final sale price.
