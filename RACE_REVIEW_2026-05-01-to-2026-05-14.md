## Root Cause Analysis: Why We Miss Winners

### 1. **WINNER MISSING FROM MODELED FIELD (67 misses, 37.4%)**
**The #1 Problem** — Winners not appearing in our analysis at all

**Examples:**
- May 7, Chester: Roman Dragon (3.5) won, but not in our field
- May 7, Chester: McQuarry won multiple races but missing from field entirely
- May 8: Multiple races at Chester had McQuarry as winner but scored 0.0
- May 8, Ascot: Secret Santa, Outback Heat appeared multiple times but missing from field

**Why This Happens:**
- **Nonrunners not being removed in real-time** — horses initially entered don't run, but our field still includes them
- **Data sync delays** — BetFair data vs our model field may not align perfectly
- **Late declarations** — some horses added/removed close to race time
- **Late scratches** — withdrawals at weigh-in not captured

**Impact:** These are automatic losses — we can't pick what we don't know about

**Action Required:**
- Implement real-time nonrunner tracking
- Build a "post-declaration" field verification (30 mins pre-race)
- Pull final field from BetFair/official sources 5 minutes before race time

### 2. **POTENTIAL IMPROVER FLAG NOISE (53 misses, 29.6%)**
**The #2 Problem** — Flagging horses as "improvers" that aren't actually in our top picks

**Examples:**
- May 7, Windsor: Zarathos (4.8) won; we picked "Kodi Fire" (scored 90) but Zarathos was potential improver in model
- May 7, Wexford: Wild Bill Hickok (13.0) won; we flagged as potential improver but picked elsewhere
- May 9, Haydock: Mohaaraj (5.5) won; scored 63, ranked 5th, but was improvement candidate
- May 12, Lingfield: Zatsgood (2.02) won; scored 53, potential improver flag but we picked "Frostmagic"

**Why This Happens:**
- **Potential improver flag is creating noise** — horses flagged as "could improve" but actually DO improve are getting deprioritized
- **Model is too conservative with improvers** — we identify them but don't give them enough credit
- **Flag vs. Score disconnect** — flagged horses have lower scores than they deserve
- **Trip suitability not weighted enough** — improvement likely but score doesn't reflect it

**Impact:** We're "hedging" on horses we think will improve, which means we pick the safer option and miss upsets

**Action Required:**
- **Recalibrate potential improver scoring** — if a horse gets the improver flag, increase its score by 15-20 points
- **Use trip suitability as a PRIMARY factor** — not secondary
- **Track improver flag hit rate** — are flagged improvers actually winning more?
- **Stop using improver flag as a "warning"** — use it as a SIGNAL
